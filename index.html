The company

Production, transmission, studios

Cairo News Company is a full HD video production company in Tahrir Square, Downtown Cairo. We cover breaking news, sport and live events for Arab and European broadcasters, and produce documentaries, commercials and features end to end.

From a stand-upper on the Corniche to a full scale feature shoot, our crews, fixers and engineers handle every phase, including the permits, the customs and the access that make filming in Egypt possible.
