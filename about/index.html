About

Twenty two years
on air from Tahrir

Cairo News Company is a full HD video production company based in Tahrir Square in Downtown Cairo, an established market leader in Middle East news production.

What we are

We have the capabilities and facilities to meet all professional TV production needs. From conception to compilation, from a stand-upper to a full scale feature film shoot, our staff of creative experts and professional freelancers can handle every phase of your production.

CNC specialises in live coverage of breaking news and live transmission, with extensive experience across sports, conferences and live events. We also offer complete production facilities for feature films, documentaries, commercials, musical, corporate, educational and industrial videos.

Since 2004 our teams have delivered coverage and transmission to Arab and European channels including CNN, BBC, France 24, France 2, TF1, beIN Sports, AVRON, Abu Dhabi TV, Dubai Sports and BFMTV, alongside several production companies.

Facilities

CNC operates 10 DSNG units, 12 camera crews and 4 live studios. The flagship studio runs five cameras against a real backdrop of the Nile, the Sixth of October Bridge, the Egyptian Museum and Tahrir Square, built for news, talk shows and live sport, with full flexibility on camera count and operating costs.

The company provides workspace for editors, producers and technicians, editing units, and complete service packages across pre-production and post-production.

Live over IP

In cooperation with NOL, CNC runs the AVIWEST DMNG service: IP transmission in HD and SD to any broadcaster worldwide. It includes the AVI STUDIO, which puts a guest on a live feed for fifteen minutes at minimal cost, and AVI feeds for pre-recorded material at affordable rates.
