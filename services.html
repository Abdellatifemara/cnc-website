Services

One call.
On air.

The complete range of production services, from the first permit to the final live feed.

01 · Transmission

Satellite, 4G and IP transmission

Live transmission from any location in Egypt and the Middle East. 10 DSNG units on standby, IT transmission and 4G LIVE, Pro180 with supporting cellular networks, and AVIWEST DMNG over IP in cooperation with NOL, reaching any broadcaster worldwide in HD or SD.

02 · Production

Camera crews and location filming

Full HD video production with 12 professional crews. Multi-camera location filming, live coverage of breaking news, sports, conferences and events, street stories, documentaries, commercials and full feature shoots. Sound engineering, lighting equipment and crews, and a complete line of grip.

03 · Studios

Five cameras over the Nile

Four live studios, the flagship equipped with five cameras and a real backdrop of the Nile, the Sixth of October Bridge, the Egyptian Museum and Tahrir Square. Full flexibility on camera count and operating costs, especially on long term contracts.

04 · Post

Editing and sound

Editing facilities and services, plus workspace for producers, journalists and editors, minutes from the story.

05 · Logistics

Permits, customs and logistics

Filming permits with easy access to antiquities and monuments, customs clearance of equipment, transportation, shipping, accommodation, and the complete line of pre-production services.

06 · Editorial

Fixers, producers and archive

Fixers and producers, translation and interpretation, analysis, and access to public figures: politicians, activists, celebrities, athletes and media experts. Archival footage of recent events including the 2011 revolution, plus historical archive. Consultation for foreign producers and start-up newsrooms.
